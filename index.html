How to Play

  1. You start with 100 points. Input the number of points you want to bid and click 'submit points' to start playing.
  2. Click 'deal cards' to deal your hand.
  3. Click the cards you want to exchange. You can click on the card again to unselect it. You do not need to click on any cards if you do not want to exchange any. Then, click on 'exchange/hold cards' to exchange/keep your cards.
  4. You will earn points = your hand score multiplied by points bidded (eg. If your hand score is 2 and you bidded 3 points, you earn 6 points). The scoring table can be found here. Repeat step 1 to begin the next round.
